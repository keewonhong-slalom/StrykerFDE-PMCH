### AI-Enabled Complaint Intake

An AI layer embedded at the point of complaint submission validates field rep MS Form responses against required TrackWise fields, surfaces data quality issues in real time, and performs early failure mode pre-classification before the complaint enters the specialist review queue. The solution targets the 40–50% of ~40K annual JR & MET complaints that require manual follow-up due to incomplete or ambiguous intake data, compressing the 90-day closure cycle and reducing regulatory audit exposure. The current fragmented flow (MS Forms → Power Automate → SharePoint → manual review → TrackWise) contains no automated validation, and the planned UIPath RPA migration is still unscoped.
