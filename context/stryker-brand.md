## Writing Do's

- Lead with the so-what before the how
- Use plain language; if technical, define the term
- Use active voice
- Quantify claims wherever possible

## Writing Don'ts

- Avoid vague superlatives ("best-in-class," "world-class") without evidence
- Do not include forward-looking financial commitments
- Do not share patient-identifiable information
