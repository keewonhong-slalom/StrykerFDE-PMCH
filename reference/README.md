# reference/

Curated source documents used to ground AI outputs in authoritative information.

## What Belongs Here

- Clinical studies and white papers
- Competitive intelligence reports
- Market research and sizing data
- Product specifications and datasheets
- Industry standards and regulatory guidance
- Analyst reports (Gartner, IDC, etc.)

## Organization

```
reference/
  clinical/         # Studies, clinical evidence
  competitive/      # Competitor analysis, market maps
  market/           # Market data, sizing, trends
  product/          # Product specs, datasheets
  regulatory/       # Compliance and regulatory docs
```

## Notes

- Files here should be treated as read-only source material
- Cite references in all outputs that use them
- Keep file names descriptive: `<source>-<topic>-<year>.<ext>`
